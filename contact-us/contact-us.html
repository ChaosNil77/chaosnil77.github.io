Parking & Access Information

We have limited parking on site. There are some pay and display parking bays on The Bayle. If these are full, please use one of the nearby public car parks.

Disabled access is available both through the playground and the Woodward Hall access. Please phone the office if you wish to make use of this. A lift is available to the first floor of the school.

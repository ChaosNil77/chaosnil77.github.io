Welcome to Class 6

Class 6 is taught by Mr. Davies with support from Mrs. Taylor. As the senior class of St. Eanswythe's, we focus on developing independence, critical thinking, and preparing our pupils for their transition to secondary school while having a fantastic final year!

Current Topic: The Ancient Maya & Sustainable Science

This term, Class 6 is exploring the history, architecture, and daily life of the ancient Maya civilization. In science, we are investigating electrical circuits and how energy efficiency impacts the modern world.
